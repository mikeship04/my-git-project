About me

Hello my name is Mike Shippy, a 35 year old career changer from Phoenix Arizona. After almost 20 years in the hospitality industry I was standing in a ballroom on new years eve bartending a private event, instead of spending some much needed time with my family. At this point I knew it was time for a change. After a lot of research and personal education on the matter I decided to get everything in order so I could attend the Flatiron School. I listened to hundreds of episodes of the code newbie podcast and heard Saron interview countless inspiring people with similar stories and backgrounds making the leap to better their situations. Knowing I was not alone in my search for something greater and ever eager for a new challenge I made the choice to quit my job so I could focus on school full time.

Although I am still a newbie, this site will be continuously updated as I complete my curriculum at Flatiron School to showcase my abilitiy to learn and execute.
